# Fuel
---
### Fuel.Get
#### Get the fuel level of a vehicle

##### `Client`
##### Lib.Fuel.Get(vehicle)

#### Parameters
- **vehicle**: number - The vehicle handle
#### Return
- **return**: number - The fuel level


---
### Fuel.Set
#### Set the fuel level of a vehicle

##### `Client`
##### Lib.Fuel.Set(vehicle, amount)

#### Parameters
- **vehicle**: number - The vehicle handle
- **amount**: number - The fuel level
